Yojana Magazine (Hindi)

About the Magazine-

Yojana is a monthly magazine published in English and many other regional languages of India. The magazine gives different shades of opinion and views on any issue and thereby presents a balanced picture. You can read the magazine online and subscribe it as well. The archive of magazine is available with issues previously published. Users can also read the magazine in Hindi, Telugu, Urdu, Bengali, Gujarati, etc.

Yojana is published by the Government of India and focuses on socio-economic issues and government policies.
