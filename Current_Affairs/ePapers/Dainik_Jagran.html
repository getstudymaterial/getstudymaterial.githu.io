History

  • The Daily Hindi Newspaper is named Dainik Jagran. By circulation, it was India’s biggest newspaper as of 2017.
  • By circulation, it was India’s biggest newspaper in 2017. It had the 17th-highest global readership in 2010.
  • Puran Chandra Gupta started the publication in 1942. The publishing firm Jagran Prakashan Limited, which is listed on the Bombay Stock Exchange and the National Stock Exchange of India, is the owner of the company.
  • In the year 1942, Dainik Jagran first appeared. The “Quit India movement” first appeared in the year that the Indian liberation fight reached its zenith.
  • During this time, Dainik Jagran was established with the mission to “Create a newspaper that would reflect the free voice of the people” according to our founder Shri Puran Chandra Gupta.
